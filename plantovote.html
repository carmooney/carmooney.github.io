Plan to Vote Flowchart

Date of Project:2020
Client: Vote Oswego
Software: Adobe InDesign

Client work for Vote Oswego. This flowchart was printed and displayed in every residence hall in SUNY Oswego as well as Lee Hall. The charts purpose is to walk through the steps of the voting process while providing helpful resources and links via QR codes.
