Vote Oswego Illustrations

Date of Project: October 2020
Client: Vote Oswego
Software: Procreate

Series of illustrations created for Vote Oswego. These illustrations were used as social media materials in the weeks leading up to the election, encouraging people to go out and vote. The phrases "Use Your Voice" and "Your Vote Counts" aim to inspire younger voters, many who are voting for the first time ever.
