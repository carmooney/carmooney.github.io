Hi, I'm Caroline

I'm an aspiring graphic designer from Westchester, NY. I’m currently in my junior year of college at SUNY Oswego where I am working towards my BFA in Graphic design and minor in Illustration. When I’m not working on projects for class you can find me working in the admissions office where I currently intern for their Admissions Promise Program and working on my commissioned work.

My recent project is my own brand CM Creatives, where I make custom stickers, prints, logos, t-shirts, cards and other designs. After graduation, I would love to continue expanding myself and my brand by going freelance and doing more commissioned work.

As a designer, I have developed many skills both in and outside of the world of graphic design. I am efficient in the Adobe Creative Suite, Procreate, and Cricut Design Space. I am also currently taking classes in html and CSS.

I would love to work with you and bring your dream project to life!
