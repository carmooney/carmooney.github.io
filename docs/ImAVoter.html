I'm a Voter Campaign

Date of Project: October 2020
Client: Vote Oswego
Software: Adobe Illustrator

Social media campaign designed for Vote Oswego. This campaign was deployed onto social media and twitter in the weeks rising up to the election. It was important to emphasize the several ways in which citizens could cast their vote, whether it be on Election Day, voting early at the polls, or casting an absentee ballot.
