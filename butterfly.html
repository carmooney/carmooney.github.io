Butterfly Stamps

Date of Project:2020
Client: Type and Image Project
Software: Procreate and Adobe Photoshop.

Project for Type and Image class. It was important to me that the butterflies I selected were unique from one another to produce a variety of colors within each composition. Symmetry was also a big factor in the project. Each butterfly was drawn on a symmetrical plane so that each side would be identical.
