Maruchan Ramen Rebrand

Date of Project: 2021
Client: Branding and packaging project
Software: Adobe Illustrator and Dimension

Packaging project that required the redesign of a package of our choice. I chose Maruchan because I have never seen this company go through any sort of rebrand. While the logo for the brand is very similar, the packaging is more modern and features illustrative elements like the ramen circling around the box.
